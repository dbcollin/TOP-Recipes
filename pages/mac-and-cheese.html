Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 500g box macaroni or other pasta
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2-3 cups shredded cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook macaroni until cooked through but firm. Allow extra time to cook in the sauce.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. And flour, salt and pepper and stir until smooth.
  3. Pour in milk slowly while stirring continuoisly. Cook and stir until smooth and bubbling.
  4. Add 2 cups cheese and stir until melted.
  5. In a baking pan, fold macoroni into sauce. Optionally, add 1 cup shredded cheese on top for a crunchy finish.
  6. Place the baking pan in an oven at 180Â°C until visibly golden.
  7. Add extra pepper to taste and serve.

Note

Keep a careful eye on the milk as it can burn easily. Ensure you stir constantly to avoid this.
