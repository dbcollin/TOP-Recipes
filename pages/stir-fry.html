Soy and Sesame Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon oil of choice
  • 250g tofu
  • 1-2 large garlic cloves
  • 1 teaspoon grated/minced ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili flakes
  • 200g baby spinach, rinsed
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds, optionally toasted
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Soy sauce to taste
  • (Optional) Scallions to serve

Directions

  1. Dice tofu and press until dry.
  2. (Optional) Marinate diced tofu in soy sauce and crushed garlic for 1-24 hours.
  3. Heat the oil over medium-high heat and add the tofu. Stir-fry until tofu is lightly coloured.
  4. Add the garlic, ginger and chili flakes. Cook until fragrant and add soy sauce to taste.
  5. Add the spinach and stir-fry until wilted.
  6. Stir in the sesame seeds and add more soy sauce to taste. Remove from heat.
  7. Drizzle in sesame oil to taste. Serve with rice or noodles.
  8. (Optional) Garnish with scallions.
