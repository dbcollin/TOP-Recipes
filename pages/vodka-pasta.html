Vodka Rigatoni

Ingredients

  • 500g box rigatoni
  • 100g shallots
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp butter / olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • 30ml vodka
  • 3/4 cup cream
  • 100g tomato paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Parmesan cheese to taste
  • (Optional) Fresh basil to serve

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook rigatoni until al dente.
  2. Finely dice shallots and crush garlic cloves.
  3. Heat butter or oil over medium heat. Add shallots and garlic, cooking until starting to brown.
  4. Add tomato paste and chili flakes and stir until shallots are coated. Continue cooking and stirring until browning on bottom of pan.
  5. Add vodka to deglaze pan. Reduce heat to low.
  6. Add 1/4 cup pasta water and heavy cream to pan. Add more pasta water or cream to taste.
  7. Remove sauce from heat and fold in rigatoni.
  8. Add parmesan to taste and fold with rigatoni in the sauce.
  9. Serve with extra parmesan to taste. Optionally also serve with fresh basil leaves.
